Who is Don Shi ?

Life

Hailing from the vibrant music scene of West Africa, Don Shi burst onto the scene with his passion for delivering powerful and uplifting music. With a passion for storytelling and a deep love for music that resonates with the soul, Don Shi quickly became captivated by the global sounds of Dancehall and Ragga, blending these genres with his own unique flair to create a sound that is fresh, dynamic, and full of energy.

In his pursuit to expand his musical horizons, Don Shi relocated to Essaouira, a coastal town in Morocco known for its rich cultural diversity and vibrant music scene. It was here that fate led him to meet European producer Kalo, whose eclectic style and love for reggae and dub music sparked an instant connection. The two immediately began collaborating, merging their different backgrounds and influences to produce tracks that are both rooted in African rhythms and infused with the global energy of reggae, dub, and dancehall. As Don Shi continues to work with Kalo and pushes the boundaries of his musical creativity, he is quickly gaining recognition for his authentic style and captivating performances. With his growing fanbase and an ever-expanding musical catalogue, Don Shi is set to make a lasting impact on the global music scene, bringing the energy of Ragga, Dancehall, and Dub music to new audiences around the world.
